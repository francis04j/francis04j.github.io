About

This site is a partial collection of projects I've worked on, stories I've written down, and software I've published.

In the past, I've worked as a software engineer, computer technician, sports captain, community treasurer and fitness coach. I used to travel a lot; I love learning new culture, meeting new people and going for adventures. I have great stories and pictures from leading a band in Rio Carnival to racing along the great wall of China. I've visited every continent on the planet. I currently live in London, UK.

I love arhitecture in buildings, computers, and softwares. I love the art and science of putting things together. This love led to my passion of software development, particularly in the areas of api integration, event sourcing, microservices, data management, privacy, and serialisation.

I enjoy athletics, running in particular. I have completed many 10Ks, Half Marathons, and Marathons. I'm a big believer that anyone can run. I often coach people to help them get into running

In general, I enjoy learning, teaching and building things.
